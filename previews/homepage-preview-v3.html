The No-Fuss Audio Guestbook

It only takes a minute!

1

Before the event

Just log in and give us the name of your couple, their email, and the date of the event - we'll do the rest!

2

The day of the event

When you sign up with us, we provide a phone that you keep at your venue. All you need to do is punch in a code we provide and the phone is set up!

3

During the event

All guests need to do is pick up the phone, listen to a message from the couple, leave a message and hang up when they're done.

4

After the event

Your couple will be able to access all of their messages instantly from their secure account on our website
