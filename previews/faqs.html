For Venues

Technical Questions

Guests can dial in remotely! Each event gets a phone number that guests can call from anywhere in the world to leave their message. Perfect for guests who can't travel or are unwell. They'll hear the same personalized greeting and can leave their message just like they were there in person.
Yes! The phone number is based in your venue's country, so guests calling from abroad will use international dialling (just like calling any number in your country). Standard international rates apply based on their phone network agreement.
Messages are stored as high-quality MP3 files. They'll play on any device and can be imported into any audio software if you want to create compilations or edit them.
No limit at all. Whether you have 10 guests or 300, all messages are recorded and stored. The phone can handle hundreds of messages in a single event without any issues.
