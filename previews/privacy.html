Privacy Policy.

Last updated: February 9, 2026

At WeddingRingRing, operated by RingRing Digital Ltd, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our audio guestbook service.

1. Information We Collect

Venue Information

When you register as a venue partner, we collect:

  • Venue name and contact details
  • Primary contact person's name and email
  • Phone number and address
  • Billing and payment information

Couple Information

When venues create events for couples, we collect:

  • Couple's names
  • Email addresses for message delivery
  • Wedding date

Audio Messages

We collect and store:

  • Audio recordings left by wedding guests
  • Timestamps of when messages were recorded
  • Phone numbers of guests who dial in remotely (for technical purposes only)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our audio guestbook service
  • Process and deliver audio messages to couples
  • Send service-related communications and updates
  • Process payments and manage venue accounts
  • Improve our service and develop new features
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All audio messages are encrypted during transmission and storage
  • We use secure servers with bank-level encryption
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security audits and updates

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data in the following circumstances:

  • With Couples: Venues share couple information to set up events, and couples receive their audio messages
  • Service Providers: We use trusted third-party services for telephony, cloud storage, and payment processing
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our legal rights

5. Data Retention

Audio Messages: Messages are stored indefinitely for couples to access. Couples can delete their messages at any time from their account.

Account Data: We retain venue and couple account information for as long as accounts remain active, plus a reasonable period afterward for legal and business purposes.

Deletion Requests: You can request deletion of your data by contacting us. Some information may be retained for legal compliance.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent at any time

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you logged into your account
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze how our service is used
  • Improve user experience

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

8. International Data Transfers

We operate internationally and may transfer data across borders to provide our service. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data regardless of where it's processed.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. Audio messages may contain children's voices as wedding guests, but we do not collect personal information about them.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or through our service. Continued use of our service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Your lawful basis for processing includes:

  • Contract: Processing necessary to provide our service
  • Legitimate Interest: Improving our service and preventing fraud
  • Consent: For marketing communications (which you can opt out of)
